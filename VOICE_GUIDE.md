## 💡 Tips

### Quality Comparison
- **WaveNet voices**: Better quality, more natural, still FREE (counts toward 4M monthly limit)
- **Standard voices**: Good quality, slightly robotic, FREE

### Speed Recommendations
- **0.75x**: Relaxing, meditative content
- **1.0x**: Normal reading speed (recommended)
- **1.25x**: Faster consumption, podcasts
- **1.5x**: Speed listening, familiar content

### Character Count
All voices count toward your **4 million character monthly free tier**:
- 100k characters ≈ 50,000 words ≈ 5-6 hours of audio
- Average user stays well within free tier!

## 🔊 How to Change Voices

1. Open the app
2. In the "Voice" dropdown, select your preferred voice
3. Click "Convert to Audio"
4. Your selection is remembered for next time!
